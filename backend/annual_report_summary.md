## Chairman/Managing Director’s Letter
*   **Key Highlights:**
    *   The company achieved a significant milestone by crossing ₹10,000 Crores in revenue for the first time.
    *   Revenue grew by 22% year-on-year, reaching ₹10,002 Crores.
    *   Profit After Tax (PAT) increased by 15% year-on-year to ₹1,250 Crores.
    *   The company maintained its market leadership position in key segments.
    *   Investments in R&D led to the launch of 5 new products, contributing to future growth.
    *   Focus on sustainability initiatives resulted in a 10% reduction in carbon footprint.
    *   The company expanded its global presence by entering two new markets in Southeast Asia.
*   **Future Outlook:**
    *   Anticipate continued growth driven by strong demand in core markets.
    *   Plan to further invest in digital transformation and automation.
    *   Committed to enhancing shareholder value through sustainable growth and operational efficiency.
    *   Expect to launch 3-4 new products in the next fiscal year.
    *   Targeting a 15-18% revenue growth for the upcoming year.
